Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving forward-thinking solutions for a smarter, more efficient future.

Sustainability

Building eco-friendly systems to protect and nurture our planet.

Infrastructure

Creating resilient foundations for growth and connectivity.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals and communities to thrive and achieve their potential.

The Vision of Future"

The Vision of Future Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

We must envision a future where every individual, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to thrive. Through innovation, sustainability, and empowerment, we can create a society free of poverty and full of possibilities.True development begins when we empower individuals to realize their potential, foster innovation to address societal challenges, and build systems rooted in equality and sustainability. By creating opportunities for everyone—especially those at the margins—we can transform poverty into progress and dreams into reality. Together, we can shape a future where hope, resilience, and human creativity become the driving forces of a prosperous and just world.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
